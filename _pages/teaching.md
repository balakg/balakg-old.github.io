<a href="https://computervision.rice.edu/">Intro. to Computer Vision</a> (Spring): Undergraduate and graduate class covering basic to advanced concepts in computer vision. Topics include image formation and cameras, signal processing, mid-level vision, and high-level vision using deep neural networks, and recent foundation models.

<a href="https://neuralsynthesis.rice.edu/">Generative AI for Image Data</a> (Fall): Graduate class covering state-of-the-art research in generative image algorithms using neural networks and rendering. Most lectures will revolve around discussing important publications in the field.
